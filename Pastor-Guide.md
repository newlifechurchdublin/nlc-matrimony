# New Life Church — Matrimony
### A simple guide for the Pastor

You have **two links**:

| | Link | For |
|---|------|-----|
| 📝 **Add a profile** | *the Google Form link* | Give to families, or fill it yourself |
| 🔍 **Search profiles** | **https://newlifechurchdublin.github.io/nlc-matrimony/** | For you & trusted helpers |

> 🔑 To open the search page you’ll be asked for a **PIN**. Type it in once.
> Share the page link **and** the PIN only with people you trust.

---

## ➕ To add a new profile
Open the **Form link** → fill in the details → **attach a clear photo** → **Submit**.
That’s all — it appears on the search page after you tap **↻ Refresh data**.

You can either fill it yourself, or just **send the Form link to the family** and let them do it.

---

## 🔍 To find a match for someone
1. Open the **search page** and enter the **PIN**.
2. Use the **search box** — type anything: a name, a job (“nurse”), a place (“Coimbatore”),
   a qualification, a church…
3. Or use the drop-downs to narrow by **Gender**, **Marital status**, or **Age**.
4. Tap any **photo card** to see the full details.
5. From there you can tap **📞 Call** or **WhatsApp** the contact straight away.

> See a new profile isn’t showing? Tap **↻ Refresh data** at the top.

---

## ✏️ To change something in a profile
Sometimes a phone number or job changes.
1. Open the **Matrimony Database** (the green Sheet in your Google Drive).
2. Click the cell you want to fix → type the new value → press **Enter**.
3. Tap **↻ Refresh data** on the search page.

---

## 🗑️ To remove a profile (e.g. they got married)
**The easy way — right on the search page:**
1. Open the page → tap the person’s **card**.
2. Scroll down → tap the red **🗑 Remove** button → confirm.
3. Done — it deletes their **row and their photo** (to save space) in one go.

That’s the simplest way and needs nothing else.

---

## A few gentle reminders 🙏
- Keep the **PIN** private — it’s the only thing protecting people’s details.
- Photos and details belong to real people; please don’t share the link publicly.
- Everything is **free** and backed up in the church’s Google account
  (`newlifekids.elearning@gmail.com`).
